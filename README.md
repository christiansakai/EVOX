##Time
At 1X speed, a second IRL equals to 4 minutes IG.
1 day ig = 1 month ig

|IG      | 1x            | 2X            | 4X    |
|--------| :------------- |:-------------:| -----:|
| 1 day  | 6 m     | 3m | 90s |
| 1 month| 6 m      | 3m     |   90s|
| 1 year| 1h12m| 36m    |    18m |
